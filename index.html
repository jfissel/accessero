01THE IDEA

Charging is the last thing technology forgot. We’re going to make it the best part of owning a device.

Gallium nitride, everywhere

GaN switches faster, runs colder, and wastes a fraction of what silicon does. We’re building on it from day one — small enough to forget, powerful enough to run your whole desk.

Power that thinks

Charging shouldn’t be dumb wattage. Onboard intelligence will read every device, learn its battery, and shape the current in real time — full speed when you need it, gentle when you don’t.

Built for decades

Recycled aluminum, replaceable parts, firmware that improves with age. The most sustainable accessory is the one you never throw away.

Built clean. Built to last.
