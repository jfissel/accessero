01THE CRAFT

Most chargers are an afterthought. Ours are the main event — machined, measured, and tuned until electricity itself feels designed.

Gallium nitride core

Half the size, twice the efficiency. Our fourth-generation GaN architecture moves 240 watts through a body smaller than a deck of cards.

Thermal intelligence

Sixteen onboard sensors sample temperature 1,000 times a second, shaping the current curve so your devices charge fast — and stay cold.

Built for decades

Unibody aluminum, replaceable cabling, firmware updates for life. We design products you keep, not products you replace.
